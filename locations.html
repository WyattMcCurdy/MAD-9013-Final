Field Locations

There are several fields in the National Capital area that host the teams of the Gunners Soccer League. Of the fields used several are found in the Greater Ottawa area such as the Nepean Hotspurs Soccer Club, the Ottawa St-Anthony Italia Soccer Club and the Cumberland United-Unis Soccer Club. Prescott also has two fields the Memorial field and Casco field. With the final league fields at Laurier hill in Brockville.
